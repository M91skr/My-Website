Introduction

I was born in Iran and I am currently experiencing the third decade of my life.

In the first two decades of my life, I was getting to know myself and the role I want to play. During these years, I thought about becoming a policeman, a reporter, and a lawyer, and along the way, I read dozens of psychological and legal books. But in the end money won and I became an engineer!! Of course, by choosing the field of industrial engineering and especially the quality branch, I realized that this field is in line with the goals of my teenage years.

I am a determined person who believes in continuous improvement. Usually, it is hard for me to go past defects and problems, unless I am sure that either there are short-term plans or long-term plans to fix them, or I can create them. This feature can reassure those around me that there is a trusted, hardworking and helpful person by their side to make improvements, and it can also make me seem a bit strict.

If you believe in the classifications made by personality tests, you can recognize me with the ISTJ personality type in the MBTI test and ISE in the Holland test. Also, according to the Bar-On test, I have an emotional intelligence of 72, which is not bad, but I can improve it by improving interpersonal relationships. Finally, according to the NEO test, in terms of mental health, I am among indifferent people, from the aspect of defense style, I am among compatible people, in terms of anger control, I am among easy-going people, from the aspect of self-control, I am among goal-oriented people, and in terms of interests, I am among introvert people. From the aspect of the style of interactions, I am classified as a humble person, in terms of the style of activities as a hardworking person, from the aspect of attitude as a progressive person, in terms of learning style as a learner and in terms of character as an effective altruistic person.

I wrote all this, but I deeply believe that man performs different behaviors according to different scenarios and changes under different conditions, and for this reason, he has gained more control over the planet than other animals. We are children of adaptation.
